About Us

Crafting Digital Experiences That Matter

We are a passionate team of designers, engineers, and strategists dedicated to turning bold ideas into polished digital products. Since our founding, we have partnered with startups and established enterprises alike — delivering solutions that are fast, accessible, and built to last. Our mission is simple: help you succeed online by creating experiences your users will love.

Our Story

My Project was born out of a shared frustration: too many businesses were settling for slow, generic websites that failed to reflect their true potential. In 2018, a small group of developers and designers came together with a clear goal — raise the bar for what a digital product can be.

What started as a two-person consultancy operating out of a co-working space has grown into a full-service studio with over a dozen specialists across engineering, UX design, and product strategy. Every project we take on is treated as a partnership, not a transaction.

Today we are proud to have shipped more than 120 projects across six years, serving clients in industries ranging from e-commerce and education to healthcare and fintech. Each engagement sharpens our craft and deepens our commitment to quality.

Our Values

Great products are never built in isolation. We work closely with clients, welcome feedback at every stage, and foster an internal culture where every voice contributes to the outcome.
